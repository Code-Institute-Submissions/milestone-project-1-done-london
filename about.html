About

Who we are

Done London was founded in 2012 and is an independent design and print studio based in South London, specialising in hand screen-printed graphic apparel and taking insperation from DIY subcultures, urban music and art scenes. It is a grass roots brand, producing casual streetwear styled garments, as well as collaborating with artists, musicians and brands such as Tizer, Kwake Bass aka DEM1NS and Patty&Bun to create limited edition printed T-shirts. Done London is one of only a few companies licenced by TfL to produce apparel.
